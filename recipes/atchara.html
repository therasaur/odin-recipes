Atchara Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 medium green papaya, peeled and shredded
  • 1 medium carrot, peeled and shredded
  • 1 small red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the shredded papaya, carrot, bell peppers, onion, and garlic.
  2. In a saucepan, combine the vinegar, water, sugar, and salt. Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring until the sugar and salt are dissolved.
  3. Pour the hot vinegar mixture over the vegetables in the bowl. Stir well to combine.
  4. Let the mixture cool to room temperature, then transfer it to a clean jar or container with a lid.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 24 hours before serving to allow the flavors to meld together. The atchara can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
